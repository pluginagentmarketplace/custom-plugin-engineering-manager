- Team size optimization
- Experience mix (senior/junior balance)

- Norming: Establishing standards
- Performing: Peak productivity
- Adjourning: Team transitions

**Effective Delegation**

- Clear expectations

- Support and checkpoints
- Learning opportunities

- Micromanagement

- Unclear deadlines
- Mixed accountability
