## How to Use Templates

1. **Download/Copy template**
2. **Customize for your context**
3. **Fill in team-specific info**
4. **Share with relevant people**
5. **Track and review regularly**
6. **Iterate and improve**

## Common Use Cases

- **Starting as a manager**: Full suite of templates
- **Building a new team**: Hiring + onboarding templates
- **Performance season**: Review + goal templates
- **Team culture issues**: Culture + communication templates
- **Making tech decisions**: Technical + architecture templates
- **Hiring round**: Recruitment full toolkit
- **Strategic planning**: Planning + OKR templates

## Template Customization Tips

- Adapt to your team size and culture
- Make templates simple and clear
- Get team input on formats
- Iterate based on feedback
- Use consistently across team
