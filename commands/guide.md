## Example Guidance

### Challenge: Difficult 1-on-1 Conversation

**Situation:** You need to give critical feedback to a talented but difficult team member

**Guidance includes:**
1. **Preparation** (before meeting)
   - Specific examples
   - Clear expectations
   - Desired outcome
   - Emotional readiness

2. **Conversation Structure** (during meeting)
   - Opening and context
   - Describing the behavior
   - Impact on team
   - Listening to their perspective
   - Moving forward plan

3. **Follow-up** (after meeting)
   - Document discussion
   - Support and coaching
   - Track improvements
   - Recognition of progress
