# /strategies - Strategic Planning & Vision

Plan technical strategies, organizational roadmaps, long-term vision, and strategic initiatives for your engineering team.

## Usage

```
/strategies
/strategies [topic]
/strategies [planning-period]
/strategies templates
```

## Strategic Planning Topics

### Team Strategy
```
/strategies team
```
Create 3-6 month team strategy covering:
- Team goals and vision
- Technical direction
- Hiring and growth plan
- Culture initiatives
- Success metrics

### Technical Roadmap
```
/strategies technical
```
Plan technical direction including:
- Product/feature roadmap
- Architecture evolution
- Technology choices
- Technical debt paydown
- Infrastructure upgrades
- Scalability initiatives

### Hiring & Talent
```
/strategies hiring
```
Develop hiring and talent strategy:
- Hiring plan and timeline
- Skill gaps to fill
- Promotion and growth opportunities
- Succession planning
- Diversity and inclusion goals
- Team composition

### Cultural Initiatives
```
/strategies culture
```
Plan cultural improvements:

- Recognition programs

### Performance Management

Create performance strategy:

### Organizational

Plan organizational evolution:

## Strategic Planning Process

### 1. Define Context
```
- Business goals and constraints
- Market opportunities
- Team strengths and gaps
- Competitive landscape
- Resource availability
- Organizational priorities
```

### 2. Assess Current State
Use `/assess-team` to understand:
- Team capabilities
- Performance
- Culture and engagement
- Skill gaps
- Technical health

### 3. Set Vision & Goals
```
- Where do you want to be?
- What success looks like
- Key initiatives
- Measurable outcomes
- Timeline
- Required resources
```

### 4. Plan Initiatives
```
- Major projects/work
- Dependencies
- Resource needs
- Timeline and phases
- Success criteria
- Risks and mitigations
```

### 5. Create Roadmap
```
- Sequencing of work
- Quarterly milestones
- Team allocation
- External dependencies
- Communication plan
```

### 6. Align & Execute
```
- Share with stakeholders
- Get feedback
- Refine based on input
- Communicate to team
- Execute and track
- Monthly/quarterly reviews

## Strategy Templates

### Team Strategy Template

```
TEAM STRATEGY - [Period]

MISSION & VISION
- Team mission: [Why do we exist?]
- 6-month vision: [Where are we going?]
- Key principles: [How do we operate?]

CURRENT STATE ASSESSMENT
- Strengths: [What's working well?]
- Challenges: [What are our challenges?]
- Opportunities: [What can we capitalize on?]
- Threats: [What risks do we face?]

STRATEGIC GOALS (3-5 goals)
Goal 1: [Strategic goal]
  - Measurable outcome: [Metric]
  - Key initiatives: [Major work]
  - Timeline: [When?]
  - Owner: [Who's responsible?]

TECHNICAL DIRECTION
- Key architectural decisions
- Technology choices
- Scalability roadmap
- Technical debt management
- Infrastructure evolution

TEAM & TALENT
- Hiring plan: [When, how many, what skills?]
- Development plan: [Skills to grow?]
- Succession plan: [Who's next?]
- Organizational changes: [Restructuring?]

CULTURE & ENGAGEMENT
- Culture goals: [What's important?]
- Engagement initiatives: [What will improve engagement?]
- Communication plan: [How do we share progress?]
- Rituals and traditions: [What will we establish?]

METRICS & SUCCESS
- Key metrics: [What will we measure?]
- Success criteria: [How will we know we succeeded?]
- Review cadence: [When do we check progress?]
- Adjustment triggers: [When do we pivot?]

RISKS & MITIGATION
Risk 1: [Description]
  - Impact: [What happens if this occurs?]
  - Probability: [How likely?]
  - Mitigation: [How do we prevent/address?]

RESOURCE NEEDS
- Budget: [Money needed]
- Headcount: [People needed]
- Tools: [Tools/infrastructure needed]
- Time: [Manager time allocation]

QUARTERLY BREAKDOWN
Q1: [Themes and initiatives]
Q2: [Themes and initiatives]
Q3: [Themes and initiatives]
Q4: [Themes and initiatives]

COMMUNICATION PLAN
- Stakeholders: [Who needs to know?]
- Frequency: [How often?]
- Format: [How do we communicate?]
- Feedback loops: [How do we adjust?]
```
