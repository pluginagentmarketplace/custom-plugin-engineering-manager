## Assessment Process

When you run `/assess`:

1. **Interactive Questions** (5-10 minutes)
   - Quick skill-check questions
   - Multiple choice format
   - Based on your answers

2. **Experience Evaluation**
   - Years of experience in areas
   - Projects completed
   - Technologies used

3. **Knowledge Check**
   - Conceptual understanding
   - Practical application
   - Problem-solving ability

4. **Learning Goals**
   - Where you want to go
   - Timeline for learning
   - Preferred learning style

## Assessment Tips

1. **Be Honest**
   - Accurate self-assessment helps us guide you better
   - No "wrong" answers

2. **Focus on Understanding**
   - We're testing concepts, not trivia
   - Apply knowledge to problems

3. **Take Breaks**
   - Assessment takes 10-20 minutes
   - Don't rush

4. **Reassess Regularly**
   - Every 3-6 months as you learn
   - Track growth and adjust goals

5. **Use Results Constructively**
   - Strengths: Build on them
   - Gaps: Plan to fill them
   - Goals: Stay motivated
